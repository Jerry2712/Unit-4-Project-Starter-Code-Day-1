Mac DeMarco

McBriare Samuel Lanyon (Mac DeMarco) Was born April 30 1990, is a Canadian singer-songwriter, producer, and multi-instrumentalist. He has released six full-length studio albums. His syle of music is described as "blue wave" and "slacker rock", or as he describes it, "jizz jazz".

Mac DeMarco's music is somewhat dirfferent from what I've listened to. Mac DeMarco's music is something that keeps me going in life, so I guess I would describe it as an inspiration, and I would absolutely recommend his music.
